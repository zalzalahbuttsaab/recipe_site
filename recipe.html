Recipe

Chocolate cake

"This chocolate cake recipe is a classic. This version of the recipe is fast and quick and your kids will love it."

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

  • Black chocolate: 200 g
  • Sugar : 150 g
  • Butter : 200 g
  • Eggs : 4
  • Flour : 80 g

Instructions

1

 Melt butter

2

 Mix eggs into flour

3

 Mix butter into mixture

4

 Mix sugar into mixture
